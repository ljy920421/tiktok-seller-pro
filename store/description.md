## Detailed Description

**Know your REAL profit on every TikTok Shop order — instantly.**

TikTok Seller Pro adds profit calculations directly into your TikTok Seller Center. No more spreadsheets, no more guessing. See exactly how much you make after ALL fees.

**What it does:**
- Shows net profit badge next to every order amount
- Shows profit margin on each product listing
- Adds a profit summary bar to your dashboard (today / week / month)
- Click any profit badge for a detailed fee breakdown

**Fees calculated automatically:**
- Referral Fee (6% US / 9% UK & EU)
- Payment Processing Fee (1.02%)
- Your Cost of Goods (COGS) — set per product or use a default
- New Seller discount support (reduced referral rate for first 30 days)

**Supported markets:**
- United States (seller-us.tiktok.com)
- United Kingdom (seller-uk.tiktok.com)
- Europe (seller.tiktok.com)

**Key features:**
- Zero setup required — install and it works
- Set COGS per product or use a default value
- Green badge = profit, Red badge = loss — spot problems instantly
- Lightweight — no impact on page performance
- Works offline — no account or login needed
- Privacy-first — your data never leaves your browser

**Who is this for?**
TikTok Shop sellers who want to know their real profit without opening a spreadsheet. Whether you sell 5 orders a day or 500, TikTok Seller Pro gives you instant visibility into your bottom line.

**Free version includes:**
- Profit badges on all orders and products
- Dashboard profit summary
- Fee breakdown modal
- Multi-market support (US/UK/EU)
- COGS management

**Coming soon (Pro):**
- CSV/Excel export
- Profit trends & charts
- Multi-store support
- AI-powered listing optimization

**Privacy:** This extension only reads page content to calculate profits. No personal data is collected. No account required. Your business data stays in your browser.
